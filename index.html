Criminal Cases:

These cases involve alleged violations of criminal law, where the government prosecutes individuals accused of committing crimes. Examples include theft, assault, and drug offenses, and the outcome may result in fines, probation, or incarceration.

Civil Cases:

Civil litigation deals with disputes between individuals, organizations, or entities. These cases encompass a wide range of issues, including contract disputes, personal injury claims, and property disputes, aiming to resolve disputes and often result in monetary compensation or injunctions.

Family Law Cases:

Family court handles matters related to familial relationships, such as divorce, child custody, child support, and spousal support. These cases focus on the legal aspects of family dynamics and aim to provide fair resolutions for all parties involved.
